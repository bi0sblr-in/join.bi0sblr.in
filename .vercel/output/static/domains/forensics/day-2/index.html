What is Steganography?

Steganography is a way of hiding information inside everyday files, like pictures or videos, so that the hidden data isn‚Äôt obvious. Unlike encryption, which scrambles data and makes it clear that something secret is being protected, steganography makes the data look normal and blends in. This means people are less likely to notice anything unusual, making it useful for secretly sharing information without drawing attention.
